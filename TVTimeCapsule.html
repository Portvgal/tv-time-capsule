Before importing

Get your TV Time GDPR ZIP

Use TV Time's self-service export before opening the file here.

  1. Open the TV Time export page Go to the GDPR self-service page and sign in with the same TV Time account you used in the app.
    Open export
  2. Request your personal data Follow the on-screen flow to ask TV Time to prepare your export. If you used Google, Facebook, or a different email before, make sure you pick the account with your actual watch history.
  3. Wait, then download the ZIP Small accounts may be quick. Long-term or heavy TV Time users should be patient: preparing the file can take 30 minutes or more. Keep the page available and check back until the download is ready.
Download the ZIP somewhere safe. TV Time Capsule reads it locally in this browser and skips sensitive account/device files.
